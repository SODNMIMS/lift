Day 1 Journey

Mind Map was used as a creative tool to list down all the possible mismatches people face while interacting with the lift, different interactions and users of the lift were branched out on the map. We explored the problem of waiting for a lift and have decided the enhace the lift decision process.

Areas to solve:
- Efficiency in taking a smart decision on the lift.
- Estimating the crowd around a lift on a floor.
- Displaying the current status of lift.

Product Vision:
Help assist people in avoiding crowded lift areas.

Minimum Viable Product:
Detect the crowd and number of people around the lift area and give current location of the lifts.

Product Features:
Display- Visual Elements: Number of people, Floor numbers, Lift numbers

Product Roadmap:
Stage 1 - Display in College
